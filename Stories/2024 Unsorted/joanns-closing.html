Campbellsville Joann Closing January 21st

Broden Woodruff | December 25th, 2023


Picture of Joann Frabrics in Campbellsville taken by Adam Whitiker


The Joann Fabrics on Campbellsville Bypass is set to close it's doors on January 21st, 2024. This is one of many Joann Fabrics locations set to close in the state due to the loss of money from the company in the past year. According to The Street, Joann "has over $1 billion in debt and saw its interest expense double over the past six months from $27.7 million to $52.1 million, the company believes it can survive. It detailed its financing picture in its SEC filing,”
