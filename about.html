About

Founded in 2022, Formerly known as 'Good Morning Dragons', Dragon Eye Media has been producing journalistic content for the Greensburg, KY area. In 2023, Good Morning Dragons became Dragon Eye Media to cover the many shows and segments that the journalism department of GCHS offers, such as Good Morning Dragons, The Monthly Dragon, Dragon Weather Authority, Dragon Talk, Dragons Sports Center, ECT. Dragon Eye Media is a part of the Green County High School Media Department.

Meet The Team
